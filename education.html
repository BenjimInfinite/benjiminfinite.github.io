Educational Prospects

Research Interests

My research interests are driven by a passion for solving complex problems and advancing knowledge in data science and its applications. Key areas of focus include:

  • Bayesian Methods: Exploring probabilistic models and inference techniques to handle uncertainty in data-driven decision making.
  • Text as Data: Utilizing natural language processing to analyze and extract insights from textual data.
  • Messy Data: Developing methods to clean and analyze imperfect or incomplete datasets.
  • Multilevel Modeling: Investigating hierarchical models to understand data structure and relationships at multiple levels.
  • Ethical AI: Studying the implications of AI technologies on society and developing frameworks for responsible AI use.

Integration with Educational Pathways: My research interests align closely with my educational goals. They will guide my focus during my Master’s degree and potentially shape my PhD and postdoctoral research. By addressing these areas, I aim to contribute to both theoretical advancements and practical solutions in the field of data science.
