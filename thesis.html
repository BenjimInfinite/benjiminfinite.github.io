B.Sc. Thesis Presentation

Developing a Control System for Profitable Strategy in the Crypto Market

Supervisors: Dr. Mohammad Bagher Menhaj, Dr. Amir Abolfazl Suratgar
Presented by: Benyamin Behbudi
University: Amirkabir University of Technology

Introduction and Problem Statement

This section outlines the motivation for developing a control system to create a profitable trading strategy in the cryptocurrency market. Key challenges include the market's volatility and the need for efficient strategies to predict price movements.

Methodology and System Development

The system is designed using a combination of control methods such as PI, LQR, and MPC. The methodology involves system identification through methods like SINDy and optimization techniques to minimize cost functions and improve performance.

Enhancing System Performance

The system's performance is enhanced through a dynamic take-profit strategy, risk management using Random Forest algorithms, and volatility-based decision-making modules. The system aims to balance profits while mitigating risks in highly volatile markets.

Results and Conclusion

The control system significantly outperformed random strategies in profitability and risk reduction. Future work includes expanding the system to handle multiple coins, reinvesting profits, and further refining risk management techniques.
