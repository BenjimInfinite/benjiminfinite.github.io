B.Sc. Thesis Presentation

Thesis Title: Developing a Control System to Achieve Profitable Strategies in the Cryptocurrency Market

Supervisors: Dr. Mohammad Bagher Menhaj, Dr. Amir Abolfazl Suratgar
Presented by: Benyamin Behbudi
University: Amirkabir University of Technology
Date: March 2024

Abstract

This thesis addresses the design and implementation of a trading system in the cryptocurrency market, aimed at maximizing profit while reducing risk. The system uses a control-based approach to predict price direction, rather than focusing solely on statistical price predictions. A combination of methodologies like SINDy (Sparse Identification of Nonlinear Dynamics) and Model Predictive Control (MPC) is applied to identify system dynamics and optimize trading strategies.

Introduction and Problem Statement

The cryptocurrency market is known for its high volatility, presenting challenges in identifying profitable trading opportunities. This project focuses on developing a system that improves profitability while managing risks by using control theory and machine learning techniques to optimize trading decisions.

  • Main Problem: High volatility in crypto markets makes it difficult to predict price movements accurately.
  • Objective: Develop a system that predicts market trends and adjusts trading strategies dynamically to maximize profits while minimizing risks.

Methodology

The system uses a multi-step approach involving both classical control methods and machine learning:

  • System Design: The control system is designed using methodologies such as Proportional-Integral (PI), Linear Quadratic Regulator (LQR), and Model Predictive Control (MPC) for optimizing trades.
  • Price Prediction: A key feature is predicting price direction (rise or fall) using binary classification models, rather than predicting exact prices.
  • System Identification: The SINDy algorithm is used to identify the system's dynamics and derive the equations governing its behavior.

Results and Conclusions

The proposed control system, optimized through MPC, performed significantly better than random strategies. Key findings include:

  • Improved profitability by dynamically adjusting the take-profit and stop-loss levels.
  • Effective management of trading risks through a combination of control techniques and machine learning algorithms like Random Forest.
  • Potential for future expansion to handle multiple cryptocurrencies and implement compounding strategies.

Future Work

The system can be further enhanced by:

  • Incorporating additional cryptocurrencies into the portfolio to diversify risk.
  • Introducing reinvestment strategies to exponentially grow the account size.
  • Implementing more sophisticated risk management techniques for handling market correlations.
